Teaching

January 1, 2023    teaching

I teach courses in statistical and computational methods at both the graduate and undergraduate levels. I also teach a course in economic sociology for undergraduates.

In the past, I’ve taught courses in data science for beginners and introductory workshops in both R and python. I also have over six years of experience in consulting on statistical analysis and research design for graduate students and faculty. I am prepared to teach courses in organizations, the logic of inquiry, and specific computational methods at all levels (including text analysis and social network analysis) and special topics related to my research (e.g. Banking and Society, Organizations and Failure, Conversation).

I also have an M.Ed. in Secondary Education from George Washington University and three years of experience in teaching high school mathematics and computing courses.
