Research

January 1, 2023    research

Organizations and Structures of Communication:

My work on the effects of communications structures uses text analysis, information theory, and social networks to investigate the relationships between interactions and social structures in conversations, discussions, and other forms of organizational communications.

Methodology

My methodological work aims to do two things: (1) resolve methodological problems encountered in my substantive work and (more interestingly) (2) understand the relation between sociological theorizing and quantitative research. In particular, I’m interested investigating how semantic theories can be substantiated with statistical modeling and how quantitative statistical research can be applied to abductive theorizing.
